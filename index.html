About Me

Jessica Melton is a sophomore at Tidewater Community College pursuing an Associate of Science in Computer Science. She plans to transfer to Old Dominion University in the fall of 2025 to pursue her ultimate educational goal of completing a Master of Science in Data Science and Analytics. Jessica has a passion for understanding and addressing societal challenges and believes that through data analytics, there is immense potential for harnessing information to drive meaningful change.


Jessica is currently participating in the Research Experience for Undergraduates internship at Old Dominion University, which is funded by the National Science Foundation. During this internship, which is centered around Disinformation Detection and Analytics, she will be researching misinformation in the Venezuela-Guyana border dispute and its impact on Venezuelan refugee sentiment and Guyanese national security. Her primary goals for the internships are to sharpen her teamwork and research skills while gaining experience with real-world datasets and analytical tools.
