Hello, world!

My name is Jessica Melton, and I am a sophomore at Tidewater Community College pursuing an Associate of Science in Computer Science. I plan to transfer to Old Dominion University in the fall of 2025 to pursue my ultimate educational goal of completing a Master of Science in Data Science and Analytics. I have a passion for understanding and addressing societal challenges and believe that, through data analytics, there is immense potential for harnessing information to drive meaningful change.

A job contributing to research and data analysis initiatives in the public health sector inspired my return to college to study computer science. Before returning to college, I worked as an Education Paraprofessional for over ten years, where I developed a track record of accurately reviewing, documenting, and resolving written and digital data pertaining to student progress and developmental milestones. I am now eager to leverage my data management skills and my experience in education into a career in data analytics.
